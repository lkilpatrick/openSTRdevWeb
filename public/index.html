The problem

Property management software was not built for operators.

Fragmented tools

Calendars in one app, messaging in another, financials somewhere else. Nothing talks to anything.

Manual coordination

Cleaning schedules, check-in instructions, maintenance requests — all handled by hand, every time.

Inflexible systems

Off-the-shelf platforms force you into their workflows. Your operation has to bend to fit their product.

Vendor lock-in

Your data, your listings, your guest history — all held hostage by a subscription you can never leave.

The solution

Software you own, built for the way you work.

Self-hosted

Run OpenSTR on your own server. Your infrastructure, your rules, your data.

Open source

Every line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, extend it. No black boxes.

Customizable

Build the workflows that match how you actually operate, not how a product manager imagined you do.

STR and long-term

Manage short-term vacation rentals and long-term leases from a single platform.

The case for ownership

Your property. Your software. Your rules.

Most property management platforms are built to keep you dependent. OpenSTR is built on the opposite principle: you should own your tools the same way you own your properties.

Read the full case for OpenSTR

No lock-in

Flexible workflows
